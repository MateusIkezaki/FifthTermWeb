Project's Description

Guarantee your safety at our anti COVID-19 staion where you can quickly santize your hand, desinfect your shoes and measure your temperature before entering Avenues campus to avoid the spread of virus. This project consists of a station that has a touchless temperature measuring system, which will tell if you have a fever and have to go to the enfermary or not, an automatic hand sanitizer dispenser and also a carpet which has disinfectant product to clean the sole of people's shoes. The main function of this project is to give an extra security to people when coming back to school, ideally this product would stay in the front of the school and all the students and staff would pass trough it so that their hands are sanitized, the sole of the shoes as well, and confirm wheter they have a fever or not. This also will help preventing the spread of the virus, making the school a safer environmet.


About our Project

Our project consists of two main systems, one is the touchless temperature measuring system and the other is the automatic hand sanitizer dispenser, which will be arranged in a structure. The touchless temperature measuring system is formed by, a ultrassonic sensor, which will see if the person is close enough to start measuring their temperature, a temperature sensor, which will be responsible to measure people's temperature, and two LED's, one red and the other green, the red means that you have a fever and you need to go to the enfermary while the green means that you are ok and can go to class. The automatic hand sanitizer dispenser consists only in a ultrassonic sensor, to see if the hand of the person is close enough to pour hand sanitizer, and a servo motor, that will pull a string which will squeeze the top of the hand sanitizer pouring the alcohol in gel on the person's hands.

Our wish for this project's future is that is implmented on campus, it would mean a lot to us, besides the good it would cause for our school. One of our biggest challenges was to build and deliver the project in a week and a half, it was very challenging.
