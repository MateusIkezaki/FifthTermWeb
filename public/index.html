What is Fifth Term?

Fifth term takes place during the last month of the school year and it's an opportunity for students to deep dive on an elective for 2 weeks. There are two cycles, with a total of 4 weeks, where students have the opportunity to work with students from other grades to produce a project.

What is this Deep Dive Elective?

In this deep dive elective, students were prompted to create devices and solutions to tackle challenges and problems that came with the COVID-19 outbreak. This design challenge resulted in many different amazing products and this website was produced to showcase them. You can click on the images below or on the three stacked lines at the upper left corner to navigate through each project.
