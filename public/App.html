Project's Description

An issue not yet (or little) tackled during quarantine is creativity. The pandemic not only got in the way of those who create on a daily basis physically but also mentally, meaning that besides the fact that everyone around the world is locked down inside of their homes, always surrounded by four walls, some have multiple tasks to do and end up physically exhausted while others are going crazy due to the lack of simple activities such as walking, talking to people and more. The mental consequences of quarantine and the crisis are inevitable in all ages, some feelings described by people from our 5th term class were: anxiety, tired, overwhelmed, uncertain, confused, frustrated, distracted, exhausted, loneliness, scared, grumpy. So it is very clear why people sometimes are not able to focus, relax and let creativity flow.

Hued arises to awaken creativity under hard conditions, with daily, simple, and effective challenges, the user, with different types of arts chosen by him, will lead his mind to a smooth guide and training process to follow creative and productive processes every day.

The app would also have great importance after quarantine where some people might suffer post-traumatic experiences of social isolation or also a regular stressful day. Art can be a helpful and powerful method to deal with negative feelings, it is a way of getting rid of anxiety, stress, and harmful feelings and produce, create and innovate at the same time.
