Project's Description

We realized that people use their hands for basically everything. Keeping in mind that the virus spread through touch, we decided to make a mechanical hand that you could use to prevent touching things. This would be a fun way that we can improve our 3D modeling and robotics skills and still help a real world problem.


About our Project

During the process of doing the first functional prototype, we tested a lot of different ways to make the mechanics of the hand work and chose the best to move on, always considering the teacher and peer's feedback. We also made the first 3Dmodel in a software Tinkercad so we could 3D print it, but we didn't have time to finish it. Our future plans are if possible to finish the final version of the hand and test it.
