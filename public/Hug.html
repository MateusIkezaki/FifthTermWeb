Project's Description

Want a hug? The hugging robot can do that! Want a friend? The hugging robot can also do that! The ESAR (Emotional Support Awesome Robot) is a robot that was designed to help children during quarantine, so they can have a soft friend for them to hug and calm down in this safe place. It is very hard for children to be kept at home for so long, and it becomes overwhelming after so much time. I believe ESAR is going to be able to lower this stress, the tension, with his soft and safe hug! S2


About my Project

I believe that this idea was all about challenging myself and creating things I like based on the coronavirus pandemic. I found it very nice being able to see my final product and enjoy it, but also look back to the process and realise how nice it was to try and do something different.
